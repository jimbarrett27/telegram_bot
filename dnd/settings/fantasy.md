# Fantasy

A classic fantasy setting for dungeon-crawling adventures. Swords, sorcery, and shadowy dungeons.

## Skills

- Acrobatics — Dodging, tumbling, and balancing
- Arcana — Knowledge of magic, spells, and enchantments
- Athletics — Climbing, swimming, and feats of strength
- Insight — Reading people and sensing deception
- Investigation — Finding clues, solving puzzles, and analysing details
- Perception — Noticing hidden details and dangers
- Persuasion — Convincing others through charm or deception
- Sleight of Hand — Picking locks, pockets, and disarming traps
- Stealth — Moving unseen and unheard
- Willpower — Mental fortitude and resisting fear or mind control

## Classes

### Rogue
A cunning trickster who lives in the shadows. Quick hands, quiet feet, and a silver tongue when cornered.

#### Skills
- Stealth (3)
- Sleight of Hand (3)
- Acrobatics (2)
- Perception (2)
- Insight (1)
- Persuasion (1)

#### Starting Items
- Lockpick Set (x1) — Well-worn but reliable
- Smoke Bomb (x3) — Creates a cloud of concealing smoke
- Dagger (x1) — A short blade, easy to conceal

### Wizard
A scholar of the arcane arts. Fragile in body but devastating in knowledge and magical power.

#### Skills
- Arcana (3)
- Investigation (3)
- Perception (2)
- Insight (2)
- Persuasion (1)
- Athletics (1)

#### Starting Items
- Spellbook (x1) — Contains basic incantations
- Mana Crystal (x2) — Restores magical energy
- Staff (x1) — A gnarled wooden staff, doubles as a weapon

### Warrior
A battle-hardened fighter. Strong, tough, and fearless in the face of danger.

#### Skills
- Athletics (3)
- Willpower (3)
- Perception (2)
- Acrobatics (2)
- Insight (1)
- Investigation (1)

#### Starting Items
- Longsword (x1) — A sturdy steel blade
- Shield (x1) — Wooden with iron bands
- Healing Salve (x2) — Mends minor wounds

### Bard
A charismatic performer who inspires allies and beguiles enemies. Knows a little about everything.

#### Skills
- Persuasion (3)
- Insight (3)
- Perception (2)
- Arcana (2)
- Acrobatics (1)
- Stealth (1)

#### Starting Items
- Lute (x1) — A finely crafted instrument
- Charm Amulet (x1) — Glows faintly when magic is near
- Healing Herbs (x2) — Can stabilise a wounded ally

### Ranger
A wilderness survivor and tracker. At home in forests, caves, and mountains. Deadly at range.

#### Skills
- Perception (3)
- Stealth (3)
- Athletics (2)
- Investigation (2)
- Willpower (1)
- Insight (1)

#### Starting Items
- Shortbow (x1) — Light and accurate
- Arrows (x10) — Standard fletched arrows
- Rope (x1) — 50 feet of hempen rope
- Rations (x3) — Dried meat and hardtack
